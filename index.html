Our StoryA Nossa História

My name is Amanda Soyer. I'm a Pisces, and I love all things sweet and creative. I decorated my very first cake in 2015, and in 2019 I had the incredible opportunity to train at the Wilton School in Chicago.

For a few years, baking and cake decorating were my favourite hobbies — something I did with love whenever I could.

In May 2024, I officially launched @byamandasoyer here in Lisbon to share my custom cakes with you.

When I'm not in the kitchen baking, you'll probably find me cooking, knitting, solving puzzles, or diving into something crafty. I just love creating with my hands.

Thank you so much for being here and supporting my little cake world — it truly means everything. I can't wait to create something beautiful and delicious just for you.

Your Baker, Amanda 🫶🏾
